This piece is a work of art I created for Teacher's Day. It depicts a young girl giving flowers to a teacher, symbolizing appreciation and respect. The inclusion of "Happy Teacher's Day" and "Guru Purnima" reflects the dual inspiration behind the artwork. In Nepal, Teacher's Day falls on September 5th, while Guru Purnima, a celebration of teachers in general, usually occurs in July. This piece could be seen as a way to honor both occasions and the important role teachers play in our lives.
